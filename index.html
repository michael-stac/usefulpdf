FAQs

Who can apply to become an affiliate?

Are my earnings limited?

Do I need to be a UsefulPDF customer first??

Becoming a customer is not a requirement to join our affiliate program but it would be helpful so that you can promote UsefulPDF effectively. At the very least, we recommend registering an account and playing around so you can get an idea of how everything works.

How long is the cookie life?

Our cookie life is 60 days. As long as your referral makes a purchase within 60 days after clicking your affiliate link (without clicking someone else's link. We do last touch attribution) then you will be credited with the referral).

When are payouts made???

Payouts are made on the 15th of the month following a thirty-day hold period. For example, if someone signs up on October 1, then the thirty days will elapse on October 31 and you’ll be paid out on November 15th. If someone signs up on October 17th, the 30 days will elapse on November 16 and you’ll be paid out on the following 15th day of the month which is December 15th.

How are payouts made?

Once you reach a minimum balance of $50, the payouts will be made to the PayPal account we have on file. Your PayPal account details can be changed at any time through your affiliate dashboard.
